  • IDE (Ingenieria De Equipos) has an experience of more than 40 years in realization of engineering of plants and particularly in engineering of equipment, processes and mechanical design. * * * * The IDE creator * * * *
    For each equipment type IDE can carry out the complete engineering including specifications, calculation memories, drawings; as well as all the tasks related to the purchase, transportation, erection and start up.

  • We used in our beginnings, for the realization of each stages of the engineering of equipment, spreadsheets that we developed. Today we also have recognized software as well as a deep knowledge of the internationally norms (ASTM, ASME, TEMA, BS, ANSI, API, AD-MERKBLATTER) making the job much more quick, reliable and economic.

  • We also carry out the evaluation of structural integrity and fitness for service of the mentioned equipment according to API 579, API 510, API 572, API 580 and BS 7910. Also analysis of tensions by means of finite element method (FEM / FEA).
  • Calculation and design of the piping system, analyzing flexibility, localized stresses and fatigue..
