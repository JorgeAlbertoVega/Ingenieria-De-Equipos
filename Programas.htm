Used calculation programs

To make the different engineering calculations we used in our beginnings spreadsheets that we made ourselves.

Coade-PVElite

Mechanical equipment design (vessels, exchangers) It is the complete solution for the design, analysis and evaluation of Pressure Vessels and Heat Exchangers.
Equipment can be designed for the most severe uses in a fast, accurate and economical way.
It has a module for the study of the aptitude for the service of existing equipment according to API 579.

NozzlePro

Located stress in vessels connections:
This program uses finite element technology to end the limitations of the WRC107, WR297 and Zick bulletins, allowing to calculate and design large connections.
Graphs and tables can be generated to visualize the tensions in the different points of the mentioned elements.

ETank, Intergraph, TANK

API type tanks
Programs for the design, analysis and evaluation of storage tanks.
They allow the rapid and complete calculation of new tanks as the evaluation of the service aptitude of existing tanks.

CadWorx Plant

For the design of new plants as well as for the review and optimization of existing plants.
The interface with CaesarII allows a redesign of the pipe sections with possible overstress.

Coade CaesarII

To analyze and evaluate pipe systems stress according to international codes and standards.
Accepts the transfer of CADWorx Plant files, ensuring that the complete geometry of the pipe system designed in CAD is taken for analysis.

HTRI

Thermal design
For heat exchanger design, including air coolers.

PipelineStudio

For connection design and simulation of gas or liquid pipe networks, both in static and transitory states.
It allows planning by modeling the operations of a pipeline network, including variables such as fluid demand and availability.

RamElements

Design and analysis of metallic structures.
For analysis and structural design with applications in the construction sector (buildings, industrial structures, structures for chemical plants, equipment bases, etc.).

Flaresim

Flare design. To evaluate and design flare for gas plants, refineries and chemical plants.
It generates the thermal radiation and noise generation models, as well as the temperature profiles within their scope.

Regulator Station

Analysis and design of regulator plants.
Size the heater and the dust separator needed for a new station and select the necessary regulating valves from the main manufacturers.
It also allows us to analyze and optimize existing regulator stations.

Pipe Flow Expert

Calculation of flow rates and pressure drops in pipes.
For the design and analysis of complex pipe networks in which the flows and pressures must be balanced to solve the hydraulic system.
